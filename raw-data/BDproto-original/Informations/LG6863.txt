Language name: CAMSA

Language number: 6863

Alternate names: Kamentza

Classification: South American, Equatorial

Comments:
Camsa is spoken in the Sibundoy valley, Putumayo region, Colombia. Influence of Spanish has introduced a pattern of voiced stop laxing in medial position and has resulted in clearly establishing /f/ as a separate phoneme.

Sources:
