Language name: KEFA

Language number: 4364

Alternate names: Gonga

Classification: Afro-Asiatic, Omotic

Comments:
Fleming states "Two or three tones are phonemic but unimportant in comparison to features of length and stress."  Vowels may appear both long and short.

Sources: 
Fleming, H.C. 1976. Kefa (Gonga) Languages. In M.L. Bender (ed.), The Non-Semitic Languages of Ethiopia. African Studies Center, Michigan State University, East Lansing: 351-76.
