Language name: PO-AI

Language number: 2405

Alternate names: 

Classification: Austro-Tai, Li-Kam-Tai

Comments:
Po-ai is spoken in Hienghene east coast, from Quenguip to Pindache and lower valleys in New Caledonia.

Sources: 
Li, F-K. 1977. A Handbook of Comparative Tai. University of Hawaii Press, Honolulu.
