Language name: Tamasheq

Language number: 4357

Alternate names: Tuareg

Classification: Afro-Asiatic, Berber

Comments:
All consonants may appear long (geminate). Prasse's description indicates that there are five long/short vowel pairs and two vowels that only occur short, but details are unclear.

Sources: 
Prasse, K-G. 1972. Manuel de Grammaire Touaregue. Akademisk Forlag, Copenhagen.
