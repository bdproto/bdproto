Language name: DANGALEAT

Language number: 4374

Alternate names: Dangla

Classification: Afro-Asiatic, Chadic

Comments:
Dangaleat is spoken in a mountainous area of central Chad near Mongo. There are 2 level tones. Vowels appear both long and short in the first syllable of words. All consonants can be geminated.

Sources: 
Fedry, J. 1977. Apercu sur la phonologie et la tonologie de quatre langues du groupe "Mubi-Karbo" (Guera) (Dangaleat-est, Dangaleat-ouest, Bidiyo, Dyongor). Etudes Phonologiques Chadiennes, Caprile.
