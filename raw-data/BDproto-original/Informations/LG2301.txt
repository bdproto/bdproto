Language name: Kharia

Language number: 2301

Alternate names: 

Classification: Austro-Asiatic, Munda

Comments:
Kharia is spoken primarily  in Ranchi district of Bihar, but also in Raigarh District of Madhya Pradesh, Sundargarh District of Orissa, Assam, West Bemngal, Andaman and Nicobar Islands.Voiced plosives are sometimes (pre-)glottalized.

Sources: 
Biligiri, H.S. 1965. Kharia: Phonology, Grammar and Vocabulary. Deccan College, Poona.

Pinnow, H-J. 1959. Versuch einer Historischen Lautlehre der Kharia-Sprache. Harrassowitz, Wiesbaden.
