Language name: DAFLA

Language number: 2508

Alternate names: Nisi, Nishi

Classification: Sino-Tibetan, Himalayic

Comments:
Dafla is spoken in Assam,  Darrang and Arunachal Pradesh in India.

Sources: 
Ray, P.S. 1967. Dafla phonology and morphology. Anthropological Linguistics 9: 9-14.
