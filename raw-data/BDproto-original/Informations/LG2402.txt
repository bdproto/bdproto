Language name: YAY

Language number: 2402

Alternate names: 

Classification: Austro-Tai, Li-Kam-Tai

Comments:
Yay is spoken in Northwestern Vietnam and a few speakers in the South in Vietnam, also in Southern Yunnan uin China. There are 6 tones on non-stopped syllables, 4 essentially level, 1 rising, and 1 falling.

Sources: 
Gedney, W.J. 1965. Yay, a Northern Tai language in North Vietnam. Lingua 14: 180-93.
