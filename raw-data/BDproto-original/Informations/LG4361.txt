Language name: BEJA

Language number: 4361

Alternate names: 

Classification: Afro-Asiatic, Cushitic

Comments:
Vowels appear long and short.  Beja has what might be described as a pitch-accent system.

Sources: 
Hudson, R.A. 1974. A structural sketch of Beja.  African Language Studies 15:111-142.

Hudson, R.A. 1976. Beja. In M.L. Bender (ed.), The Non-Semitic Languages of Ethiopia. African Studies Center, Michigan State University, East Lansing: 97-132.
