Language name: PAYA

Language number: 6854

Alternate names: 

Classification: South American, Chibchan

Comments:
Paya is spoken in the Olancho department of Honduras.  It is possible that some diphthongs and long vowel segments should be recognized.  /d,g/ occur in recent Spanish loans.

Sources: 
Holt, D.G. 1986. The Development of the Paya Sound-System. Ph.D. dissertation.  University of California, Los Angeles.
