Language name: MOGHOL

Language number: 2174

Alternate names: 

Classification: Ural-Altaic, Mongolian

Comments:
Moghol is spoken by older people in a couple of villages near Herat, Afghanistan. The voiced palato-alveolar affricate is described as a voiced stop followed by a voicedless fricative - this seems unlikely.

Sources: 
Weiers M. 1971. Die Sprache der Moghol der Provinz Herat in Afghanistan (Sprachmaterial, Grammatik, Wortliste). Westdeutscher Verlag, Opladen, W. Germany.
