Language name: Bandjalang

Language number: 8368

Alternate names: 

Classification: Australian, Pama-Nyungan

Comments:
/dZ/ is described by Cunningham (1969) as "identical with English "j"".  All vowels are said to occur long and short but /e/ is almost always found long.

Sources: 
Cunningham, M.C. 1969. A Description of the Yugumbir Dialect of Bandjalang (University of Queensland Faculty of Arts Papers 1/8). University of Queensland Press, Brisbane.
