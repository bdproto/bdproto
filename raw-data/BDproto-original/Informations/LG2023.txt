Language name: NEPALI

Language number: 2023

Alternate names: 

Classification: Indo-European, Indic

Comments:
Nearly all sequences of the six oral vowels are reported; syllabification of such sequences seems to be variable.  Nasalized nonlow vowels are less clearly contrastive than the low one -- some words show variation between final nasalized vowel and final nasal consonant.

Sources: 
Bandhu, C.M., Dahal, B.M., Holzhausen, A., Hale, A. 1971. Nepali Segmental Phonology.  Summer Institute of Linguistics, Tribhuvan University, Kirtipur.
