Language name: YIDINY

Language number: 8370

Alternate names: 

Classification: Australian, Pama-Nyungan

Comments:
All three vowels may appear long in non-initial syllables. "Stops are almost always voiced. Partly voiced allophones are sometimes encountered word-initially." (Dixon 1977: 32)

Sources: 
Dixon, R.M.W. 1977. A Grammar of Yidiny. Cambridge University Press, Cambridge, London, New York.
