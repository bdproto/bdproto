Language name: LUO

Language number: 4205

Alternate names: 

Classification: Nilo-Saharan, E. Sudanic

Comments:
? occurs principally as a predictable prevocalic element.  Luo has vowel harmony opposing i,e,o,u to the remaining vowels.  Gregersen posits three level tones but two basic tones are more likely.

Sources:
