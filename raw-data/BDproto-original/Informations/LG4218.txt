Language name: BERTA

Language number: 4218

Alternate names: 

Classification: Nilo-Saharan, Berta

Comments:
Triulzi et al. report all vowels appear long and geminate consonants also occur.  Tone and stress may both be contrastive.

Sources: 
Triulzi, A., Dafallah, A.A., and Bender, M.L. 1976. Berta. In M.L. Bender (ed.), The Non-Semitic Languages of Ethiopia. African Studies Center, Michigan State University, East Lansing: 513-32.
