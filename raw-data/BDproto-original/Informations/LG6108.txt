Language name: AHTNA

Language number: 6108

Alternate names: Ahtena

Classification: Na-Dene, Athabaskan

Comments:
Mentasta dialect.  Vowels appear both short and long.

Sources: 
Kari, J.  1979.  Athabaskan verb theme categories: Ahtna. Alaska native language center research papers, No. 2. Fairbanks, Alaska.

Kari, J., Buck, M.  1975.  Ahtna noun dictionary.  Alaska native language center, Fairbanks, Alaska.
