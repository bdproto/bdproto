Language name: Nubian

Language number: 4206

Alternate names: Nobiin, Mahas

Classification: Nilo-Saharan, E. Sudanic

Comments:
All vowels also occur long.  The affricate tS almost always occurs geminated, and might be treated as the geminate counterpart of dZ which is never geminate. Bell (1970) suggests a two level tone system for Nobiin.

Sources: 
Bell, H. 1968. The tone system of Mahas Nubian, Journal of African Languages, 7/1: 26-32.

Bell, H. 1971. The phonology of Nobiin Nubian. African Language Review 9: 115-39.

Stevenson, R.C. 1957. A survey of the phonetics and grammatical structure of the Nuba mountain languages IV. Afrika und Ubersee 41: 27-65.
