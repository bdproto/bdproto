Language name: SONGHAI

Language number: 4200

Alternate names: 

Classification: Nilo-Saharan, Songhai

Comments:
Prost description of vowels suggests the possibility of a larger number of mid vowels. Nasalized vowels occur without an adjoining nasal consonant only in a few uncommon words.

Sources: 
Prost, A. 1956. La Langue Songay et ses Dialectes (Memoires de l'Institut francais d'Afrique Noire 47). IFAN, Dakar.

Williamson, K. 1967. Songhai Word List (Gao Dialect). Research Notes, Department of Linguistics and Nigerian Languages, University of Ibadan, 1/3: 1-34.
