Language name: AIZI

Language number: 4131

Alternate names: 

Classification: Niger-Kordofanian, Kru

Comments:
Vowel harmony based on tongue root position is prevalent in Aizi.  /a/ is neutral and /i,e,o,u/ are opposed to the remaining vowels.  Aizi is tonal but the number of tones is uncertain.

Sources: 
Herault, G. 1971. L'Aizi: Esquisse phonologique et enquete lexicale.  Institut de Linguistique Appliquee, Unversite d'Abidjan, Abidjan.
