Language name: Angas

Language number: 4367

Alternate names: 

Classification: Afro-Asiatic, Chadic

Comments:
Angas has 3 level tones.  Burquest reports many consonants with a labialized offglide.  These are treated as Cw clusters.  Palatalization only occurs with bilabials and, under an analysis which treats the palatals as palatalized velars, with velars and is treated as a segmental feature.

Sources: 
Burquest, D.A. 1971. A Preliminary Study of Angas Phonology. Institute of Linguistics, Zaria.
