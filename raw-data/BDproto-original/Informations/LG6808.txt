Language name: KAINGANG

Language number: 6808

Alternate names: 

Classification: South American, Macro-Ge

Comments:
Kaingang is spoken in Parana state, Brazil. The series given here as prenasalized stops are pronounced as such in (1) word-medial syllable-initial position unless a nasalized vowel precedes, and (2) in syllable-final position unless a nasalized vowel precedes. In these positions with a preceding nasalized vowel and word-initially with a following nasalized vowel, simple nasalized consonants occur.  Medially between oral vowels an oral stop onset occurs (i.e. VbmbV etc.).
