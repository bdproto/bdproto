Language name: EVEN

Language number: 2167

Alternate names: Lamut

Classification: Ural-Altaic, Tungus

Comments:
Even is spoken in the Yakut ASSR and the Kamchatka Peninsula of the USSR and along the Okhotsk Artic coast. All simple vowels can appear long.  Low vowel /a_/ is grouped with the pharyngealized vowels to form one set of harmonizing vowels.  /k/ is uvular before this set of vowels.
