Language name: Korean

Language number: 2170

Alternate names: 

Classification: Ural-Altaic, Korean

Comments:
Korena is spoken  primarily in Korea. /l/ alternates with initial /r/.  Vowel length can be derived through combinations of vowels. Approximants /j,w/ precede (some) non-homorganic vowels.
