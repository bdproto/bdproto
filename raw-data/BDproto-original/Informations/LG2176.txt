Language name: DAGUR

Language number: 2176

Alternate names: 

Classification: Ural-Altaic, Mongolian

Comments:
Dagur is spoken in settlements in Inner Mongolia, in the Nen valley in Heilongjang and in northwest Xinjiang, China.

Sources: 
Bao, Su-qun ed. 1982. Da-gon-er yu jianzhi. A brief guide to the Da-gon-er (Dagur) language. Minzu Chubanshe, Beijing.
