Language name: KPELLE

Language number: 4103

Alternate names: 

Classification: Niger-Kordofanian, Mande

Comments:
Voiced plosives and fricatives can be morphophonemically derived (in initial position following a low-toned nasal, which deletes). Kpelle has three surface level tones.

Sources: 
Hyman, L.M. 1973. Notes on the history of Southwestern Mande. Studies in African Linguistics 4: 183-96.

Welmers, W.E. 1962. The phonology of Kpelle. Journal of African Languages 1: 69-93.

Welmers, W.E. 1973. African Language Structures. University of California Press, Berkeley.
