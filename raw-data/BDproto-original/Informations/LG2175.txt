Language name: UZBEK

Language number: 2175

Alternate names: 

Classification: Ural-Altaic, Turkic

Comments:
Tashkent dialect. Uzbek is spoken primarily in Uzbekistan but also in adjoining republics of the USSR and neighbouring countries. More russified forms of speech contain additional loan segments.

Sources: 
Sjoberg, A.F. 1963. Uzbek Structural Grammar. Indiana University, Bloomington.

Sjoberg, A.F. 1962. The phonology of standard Uzbek. American Studies in Altaic Linguistics (Nicholas Poppe, ed.) Indiana University Publications, Uralic and Altaic Series, vol. 13.
