Language name: AZERBAIJANI

Language number: 2159

Alternate names: 

Classification: Ural-Altaic, Turkic

Comments:
Azerbasijani is spoken in the east and west of Azerbaijan in Iran and also in Afganistan, Iraq, Syuria, Turkey, and USSR. 

Sources: 
Householder, F. W. 1965. Basic course in Azerbaijani (Indiana University Publications, Uralic and Altaic Series 45). Indiana University, Bloomington.
