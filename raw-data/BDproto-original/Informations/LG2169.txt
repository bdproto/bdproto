Language name: Manchu

Language number: 2169

Alternate names: 

Classification: Ural-Altaic, Tungus

Comments:
Manchu is spoken in few villagers in Aihui and Fuyu counties in HeilonJiang, Jilin and Liaoning Provinces in Beijing in China.

Sources: 
Austin, W. 1962. The phonemics and morphophonemics of Manchu. In N. Poppe (ed.), American Studies in Altaic Linguistics (Indiana University Publications, Uralic and Altaic Series 13). Indiana University, Bloomington: 15-22.
