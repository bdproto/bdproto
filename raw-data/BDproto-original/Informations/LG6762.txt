Language name: NAHUATL

Language number: 6762

Alternate names: Aztec

Classification: N. American, Uto-Aztecan

Comments:
North Puebla dialect.  Vowels appear both long and short.

Sources: 
Brockway, E. 1963. The phonemes of North Puebla Nahuatl. Anthropological Linguistics 5.3:14-18.

Law, H. 1955. The phonemes of Isthmus Nahuat.  El Mexico Antiguo 8:267-278.

Schumann, O., Garcia de Leon, A. 1966. El dialecto nahual de Almomoloa.  Tlalocan 5:178-192.
