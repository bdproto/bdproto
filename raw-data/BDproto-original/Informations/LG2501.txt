Language name: Taishan

Language number: 2501

Alternate names: 

Classification: Sino-Tibetan, Sinitic

Comments:
Taishan is spoken primarily in Guangdong (except for the Hakka speaking areas in the northeast the Min Nan speaking areas of the east at points along the coast as well as Hainan Island) and in the Southern part of Guangxi in china.
