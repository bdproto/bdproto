Language name: CAYUVAVA

Language number: 6840

Alternate names: 

Classification: S. American, Equatorial

Comments:
Initial /b,d/ have optional prenasalization. These and /dZ/ and /B/ are also prenasalized after nasalized consonants. Key analyzes /j,w/ as allophones of /i,u/ predictable from stress facts, but examples given conflict with his rule.

Sources: 
Key, H. 1961. Phonotactics of Cayuvava. International Journal of American Linguistics 27/2: 143-50.
