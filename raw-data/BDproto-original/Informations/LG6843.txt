Language name: YUCUNA

Language number: 6843

Alternate names: 

Classification: S. American, Macro-Arawakan

Comments:
/s/ and /h/ are in free variation in most words. /w/ may be a bilabial approximant. Nasalization of vowels seems to be associated with /h/.

Sources: 
Schauer, S. and J. 1967. Yucuna phonemics. Phonemic Systems of Colombian Languages. Summer Institute of Linguistics, Norman.
