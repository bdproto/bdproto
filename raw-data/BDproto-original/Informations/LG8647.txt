Language name: WEST MAKIAN

Language number: 8647

Alternate names: 

Classification: Papuan, West Papuan

Comments:
West Makian is spoken in the western half of Makian Island and some nearby islands to the west of the coast of South Halmahera, Maluku, Indonesia. Vowel sequences are common, including of like vowels. Most words are stressed on the penultimate syllable.

Sources:
