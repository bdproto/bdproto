Language name: Somali

Language number: 4358

Alternate names: 

Classification: Afro-Asiatic, Cushitic

Comments:
All vowels may appear long and short. Long rounded vowels are not centralized and long /I/ is indistinguishable from long /i/.  There is vowel harmony, possibly based on pharyngeal cavity size (Farnetani, 1981).
