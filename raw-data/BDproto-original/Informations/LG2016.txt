Language name: HINDI-URDU

Language number: 2016

Alternate names: 

Classification: Indo-European, Indic

Comments:
All (or almost all) consonants appear geminate. Long nasalized vowels occur only with a following nasal segment when a voiced obstruent follows.

Sources:
