Language name: MOVIMA

Language number: 6860

Alternate names: 

Classification: South American, Macro-Tucanoan

Comments:
Movima is spoken in the Santa Ana region of Beni department, Bolivia. Judy & Judy (1962) also recognize two "glottalized nasals" which only occur as codas often as phonetic glottal stop + syllabic nasal. They are interpreted as sequences /?m, ?n/. Stress is usually penultimate.

Sources: 
Judy, R. and Judy J.E. de, 1962. Fonemas del Movima: con atencion especial a la serie glotal. Notas Linguisticas de Bolivia, no. 5. SILV, Cochabamba.
