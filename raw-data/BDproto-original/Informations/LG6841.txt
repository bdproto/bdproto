Language name: HUARI

Language number: 6841

Alternate names: 

Classification: S. American, Macro-Tucanoan

Comments:
Kanke also lists lower mid vowels in her "alphabet" but these seem to be predictable variants in her wordlist. Many vowel sequences occur: her one long vowel /a:/ is interpreted as a vowel sequence here.

Sources: 
Hanke, W. 1956. Beobachtungen uber den Stamm der Huari (Rio Corumbiara) Brasilien. Archiv fur Volkerkunde, Band XI: 67-82.
