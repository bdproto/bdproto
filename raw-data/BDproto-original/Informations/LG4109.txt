Language name: Temne

Language number: 4109

Alternate names: 

Classification: Niger-Kordofanian, West Atlantic

Comments:
Temne has a basic two-level tone system. In most positions [d] can be regarded as a variant of /r/.

Sources: 
Dalby, D. 1966. Lexical analysis in Temne with an illustrative wordlist. Journal of West African Languages 3\2: 5-26.

Wilson, W.A.A. 1961. An Outline of the Temne Language. School of Oriental and African Studies, University of London, London.
