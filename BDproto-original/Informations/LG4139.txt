Language name: NONI

Language number: 4139

Alternate names: 

Classification: Niger-Kordofanian, Bantoid

Comments:
Noni has 3 level tones.  All two-tone sequences apart from MH also occur on monosyllables. Short /U/ conditions a labio-dental fricative offglide from a preceding stop.

Sources: 
Hyman, L.M. 1981. Noni Grammatical Structure, with special rreference to verb morphology. Southern California Occasional Papers in Linguistics no.9
