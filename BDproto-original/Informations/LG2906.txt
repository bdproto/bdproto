Language name: TULU

Language number: 2906

Alternate names: 

Classification: Dravidian, So. Dravidian

Comments:
Stops occur aspirated in some words on some occasions. Bhat says this is perhaps due to influence from Sanskrit; some of the examples are clearly Indic loan words, which perhaps sometimes receive a "learned" pronunciation.

Sources: 
Bhat, D.N.S., 1967. Descriptive analysis of Tulu. (Buildig Centenary and Silver Jubilee Series, 15). Poona-Deccan College Postgraduate and Research Institute.
