Language name: YORUBA

Language number: 4137

Alternate names: 

Classification: Niger-Kordofanian, Kwa

Comments:
Yoruba has three level tones.  A limited vowel harmony system operates, banning co-occurrence of higher-mid with lower-mid vowels.  /n/ can be treated in most cases as the allophone of /l/ before a nasalized vowel.

Sources: 
Bamgbose, A. 1966. A grammar of Yoruba.  Cambridge University Press, Cambridge.
