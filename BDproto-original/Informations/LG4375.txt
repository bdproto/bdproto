Language name: TERA

Language number: 4375

Alternate names: 

Classification: Afro-Asiatic, Chadic (Wuyo dialect)

Comments:
Tera is spoken in an area east of Gombe in northern Nigeria. Tera has 3 level tones.

Sources: 
Newman, P. 1970. A grammar of Tera. UCPL no. 57. University of California Press, Berkeley and Los Angeles.
