Language name: IK

Language number: 4208

Alternate names: Teuso

Classification: Nilo-Saharan, E. Sudanic

Comments:
The voiceless vowels are restricted to word-final position, but devoicing does not seem to be predicatble from any other factor.  There may be three level tones.

Sources: 
Heine, B. 1975b. Ik - eine ostafrikanische Restsprache. Afrika und Ubersee 59: 31-56.
