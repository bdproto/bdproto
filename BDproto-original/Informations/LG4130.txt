Language name: AZANDE

Language number: 4130

Alternate names: Zande

Classification: Niger-Kordofanian, Ubangi (Eastern)

Comments:
Azande has two basic level tones.  Vowel harmony opposes close "hollow" vowels i,u,3 to open "hard" vowels I,E,a,O,U.

Sources: 
Tucker, A.N. and Hackett, P.E. 1959. Le Groupe Linguistique Zande (Annales du Musee Royal de l'Afrique Centrale, Serie en 8, Sciences Humaines, 22). Musee Royale de l'Afrique Centrale, Tervuren.
