Language name: KLAO

Language number: 4133

Alternate names: 

Classification: Niger-Kordofanian, Kru

Comments:
Nasal consonants surface as the allophones of voiced plosives before nasalized vowels.  [l] is the non-initial allophone of /d/.  "e", "o", and a and their nasalized counterparts are [-ATR] according to Singler.

Sources: 
Singler, J.V. 1979. The segmental phonology of verb suffixes in Talo Klao (Kru).  M.A. Thesis.  University of California, Los Angeles.
