Language name: RESIGARO

Language number: 6838

Alternate names: 

Classification: S. American, Macro-Arawakan

Comments:
Resigaro has a two level tone system. Sequences of like or unlike vowels may bear differing tones. Only /?/ may appear syllable finally. Voiced plosives but not not affricates have optional prenasalization "particularly in initial position or for emphasis". /w/ and /j/ have voiced fricative allophones.

Sources: 
Allin, T.R. 1976. A Grammar of Resigaro. Summer Institute of Linguistics, Horsleys Green, High Wycombe, Buckinghamshire, England.
