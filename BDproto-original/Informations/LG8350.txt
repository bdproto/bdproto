Language name: Maung

Language number: 8350

Alternate names: 

Classification: Australian, Yiwaidjan

Comments:
Maung plosives are "voiceless lenes". They "tend to be voiced in the pre-stop position and voiceless in the post-stop and word-final position.  Following the nasals they are voiced, and elsewhere they freely vary between voiced and voiceless."  (Capell and Hinch, 1970:21).

Sources: 
Capell, C. and Hinch, H.E. 1970. Maung Grammar. Mouton, The Hague.
