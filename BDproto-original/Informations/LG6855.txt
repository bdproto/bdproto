Language name: SHIRIANA

Language number: 6855

Alternate names: 

Classification: South American, Chibchan

Comments:
Nasalized vowels occur in nasalized feet, in which all vowels must be nasalized.  /j/ and /r/ also have nasalized allophones in these positions but nasalization of /w/ is not mentioned.

Sources: 
Migliazza, E. and Grimes, J. 1961. Shiriana phonology. Anthropological Linguistics 3.6:31-44.
