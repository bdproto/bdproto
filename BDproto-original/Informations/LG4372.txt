Language name: DAHALO

Language number: 4372

Alternate names: 

Classification: Afro-Asiatic, Cushitic

Comments:
Dahalo is spoken in areas on the coast of Kenya north of Mombasa. Vowels occur both short and long; most consonants can be geminated. The language has a two-level tone system.

Sources: 
Tucker, A.N., Bryan M.A. and Woodburn, J. 1977. The East African click languages: a phonetic comparison. In W. Moehlig, F. Rottland, B. Heine (eds.), Zur Sprachgeschichte und Ethnohistorie in Afrika. Neue Beitraege afrikanistischer Forschungen.  Dietrich Reimer Verlag, Berlin.

Nurse, D. 1986. Reconstruction of Dahalo history through evidence from loanwords.  From Sprache und Geschichte in Afrika. vol. 7/2: 267-305.
