Language name: SEBEI

Language number: 4209

Alternate names: Sapiny, Kupsapiny

Classification: Nilo-Saharan, E. Sudanic

Comments:
The overshort vowels are described as reduced and centralized.  They do not occur word-finally but are contrastive elsewhere.

Sources: 
Montgomery, C. 1970. Problems in the development of an orthography for the Sebei language of Uganda. Journal of the Language Association of East Africa 1: 48-55.
