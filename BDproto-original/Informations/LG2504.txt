Language name: XIAMEN

Language number: 2504

Alternate names: Amoy

Classification: Sino-Tibetan, Sinitic

Comments:
Xiamen is spoken in the southern Fujian, Guang-Dong, south Hainan Island, southern Zheijiang in China.

Sources: 
(Hanyu Fangyan Gaiyao) 1960. [Outline of Chinese Dialects]. Wenzi Gaige Chubanshe, Beijing.
