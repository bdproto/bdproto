Language name: HUAVE

Language number: 6716

Alternate names: 

Classification: North American, Penutian

Comments:
San Francisco del Mar dialect.  Huave is spoken in southeastern coastal areas of Oaxaca, Mexico.  "Dental" consonants are palatalized before front vowels or glides; word-final palato-alveolars are analyzed by Suarez (1975) as followed by a semi-vowel.  Huave has a two-level tone system with little functional load.  Vowels can be long and short.

Sources: 
Kreger, G.A.S. and Scharfe de Stairs, E.F. 1981. Diccionario Huave de San Mateo del Mar. Serie de Vocabularios y Diccionarios Indigenas "Mariano Silva y Aceves", Num. 24.  Instituto Linguistico de Verano, Mexico, D.F.

Suarez, J.A. 1975. Estudios Huaves. Coleccion cientifica: Linguistica 22. Instituto Nacional de Antropologia e Historia, Mexico.
