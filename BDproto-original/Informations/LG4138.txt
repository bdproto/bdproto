Language name: BOBO-FING

Language number: 4138

Alternate names: Sya, Bobo

Classification: Niger-Kordofanian, Mande

Comments:
Bobo is spoken in western Burkina-Faso (formerly Upper Volta). The language has 3 level tones, which combine into four contours (HL, ML, LH, LM).

Sources: 
le Bris, P. and Prost, A. 1981. Dictionnaire Bobo-Fing (precedee d'une introduction grammaticale et suivi d'un lexique francais). SELAF, Paris.

Morse, M.L.A. 1976. A Sketch of the Phonology and Morphology of Bobo (Upper Volta). PhD thesis, Columbia University, New York.
