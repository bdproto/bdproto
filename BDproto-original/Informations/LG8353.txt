Language name: NUNGGUBUYU

Language number: 8353

Alternate names: 

Classification: Australian, ungrouped

Comments:
Plosives "in word initial and preconsonantal positions ... are usually voiceless and unaspirated. In intervocalic and postconsonantal positions they vary freely between voiceless and voiced unaspirated.  In word final position ... [they are] between voiceless aspirated and voiceless unaspirated."  (Hughes and Leeding, 1971).

Sources: 
Hughes, E.J. and Leeding, V.J. 1971. The phonemes of Nunggubuyu. Papers on the Languages of Australian  Aboriginals (Australian Aboriginal Studies 38): 72-81.
