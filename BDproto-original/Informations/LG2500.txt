Language name: Mandarin

Language number: 2500

Alternate names: 

Classification: Sino-Tibetan, Sinitic

Comments:
Mandarin is spoken in all of north of Changjiang River, a belt south of the Changjian from Qiujiang (Jiangxi) to Zhenjiang (jiangsu), Hubei except the southeastern corner Sichuan, Yunnan, Guizhou, the northwestern part of Guangxi and the northwestern corner of Hunan.
