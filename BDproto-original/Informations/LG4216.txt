Language name: YULU

Language number: 4216

Alternate names: 

Classification: Nilo-Saharan, C. Sudanic

Comments:
Boyeldieu posits a tone system with four level tones.  Vowel length is treated as distinctive; consonants are shorter after long vowels.

Sources: 
Boyeldieu, P. 1987.  Les Langues fer ("Kara") et Yulu du Nord Centrafricain. Esquisses Descriptives et Lexiques. Laboratoire de Langues et Civilisations a Tradition Orale, Paris.
