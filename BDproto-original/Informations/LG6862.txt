Language name: GUAMBIANO

Language number: 6862

Alternate names: 

Classification: South American, Paezan

Comments:
Guambiano is spoken around Silvia, department of Cauca, Colombia. Caudmont (1954) treats affricates as stop + fricative sequences but no other syllable-initial clusters occur; however, affricates do not occur syllable-finally as all other consonants except /kw/ do.

Sources: 
Branks, T. and Branks, J. 1973. Fonologia del Guambiano. Sistemas Fonologicos de Idiomas Colombianos, II. (SIL, Loma Linda, Colombia).

Caudmont, J. 1954. Fonologia del Guambiano. Revista Colombiana de Antropologia 5: 189-206.
