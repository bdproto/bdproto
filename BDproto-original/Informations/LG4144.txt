Language name: BETE

Language number: 4144

Alternate names: 

Classification: Niger-Kordofanian, Kru

Comments:
Werle and Gbalehi analyze Bete as having four level tones. Bete has a vowel harmony system (actually based on tongue root position) in which the lowered high and lower mid vowels do not combine with fully high or higher mid vowels; /a/ is neutral to vowel harmony.

Sources: 
Werle, J-M. and Gbalehi, D.J. 1976. Phonologie et morphonologie du Bete de la Region de Guiberoua. Institut de Linguistique Appliquee, Universite d'Abidjan.
