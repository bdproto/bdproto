Language name: NENETS

Language number: 2156

Alternate names: Yurak

Classification: Ural-Altaic, Samoyed

Comments:
Nenets is spoken in the northwest Siberia, USSR. The areas  include Tundra area from mouth of northen Dvinain northeastern Europe to delta of Yenisei in Asia, a scatering on the Kola Peninsula
