Language name: Yakut

Language number: 2161

Alternate names: 

Classification: Ural-Altaic, Turkic

Comments:
Yakut is spoken in Yakut ASSR, near the Arctic Ocean, nearly the entire length of the basin of the middle Lena River, Adan and Kolyma Rivers.
