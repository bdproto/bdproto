Language name: Kala Lagaw Ya

Language number: 8365

Alternate names: Mabuiag, Western Torres Straits

Classification: Australian, Pama-Nyungan

Comments:
Kala Lagaw Ya is spoken in the western Torres Straits Islands, north of Cape York, Australia. Vowel sequences ending with /i/ or /u/ are said to be single syllabic peaks but to be two segments (Kennedy 1981).

Sources: 
Kennedy, R.J. 1981. Phonology of Kala Lagaw Ya in Saibai dialect. Work Papers of SIL-AAB (Summer Institute of Linguistics -- Australian Aborigines Branch, Darwin). Series A, 5: 103-137.

Wurm, S.A. 1972. Languages of Australia and Tasmania. Mouton, The Hague.
