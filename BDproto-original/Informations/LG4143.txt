Language name: SANGO

Language number: 4143

Alternate names: 

Classification: Niger-Kordofanian, Adamawa.

Comments:
Sango has 3 level tones. The segments analyzed here as palato-alveolars are described as "similar to but not identical with" English palato-alveolars.

Sources: 
Samarin. 1967. Basic course in Sango.

Samarin. 1967b. A grammar of Sango.
