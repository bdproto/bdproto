Language name: IATE

Language number: 6870

Alternate names: Fulnio

Classification: South American, Macro-Ge

Comments:
Iate is spoken in Pernambuco, Brazil. It is not clear from Lapenda's description whether more of the complex consonants could be analyzed as sequences.

Sources: 
Lapenda, G. 1968. Estrutura da Lingua Iate: Falada pelos indios Fulnios em Pernambuco. Universidade Federal de Pernambuco, Recife.
