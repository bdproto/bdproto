Language name: AKAWAIO

Language number: 6853

Alternate names: 

Classification: South American, Macro-Carib

Comments:
Akawaio is spoken in the Mazanini river basin in Guyana. /s,z/ are palato-alveolar affricates adjacent to /i/.

Sources: 
Edwards, W.F. 1978. Some synchronic and diachronic aspects of Akawaio phonology. Anthropological Linguistics 20: 77-84.
