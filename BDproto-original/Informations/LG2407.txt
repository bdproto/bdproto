Language name: ATAYAL

Language number: 2407

Alternate names: 

Classification: Austro-Tai, Atayalic

Comments:
Atayal is spoken in the mountains in the North, South of the Ketagalam area in taiwan. Long /i:/ and /u:/ could be interpreted as /ij/ or /uw/.

Sources: 
Egerod, S. 1966. A statement on Atayal phonology. Artivus Asiae, Supplement 23: 120-30.
