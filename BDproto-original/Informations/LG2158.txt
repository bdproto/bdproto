Language name: TURKISH

Language number: 2158

Alternate names: Osmanli

Classification: Ural-Altaic, Turkic

Comments:
Turkish is spoken in Turkey, Bulgaria, Cyprus, USSR, Greece and others.

Swift, L.B. 1963. A Reference Grammar of Modern Turkish (Indiana University Publications, Uralic and Altaic Series 19). Indiana University, Bloomington.

Underhill, R. Turkish Grammar (manuscript). San Diego State University
