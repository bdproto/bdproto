Language name: TIRURAY

Language number: 2431

Alternate names: 

Classification: Austro-Tai, W. Malayo-Polynesian

Comments:
Tiruray is spoken in Cotabato province, south-west Mindanao, Philippines. /w/ is described by Post (1966) as a 'bilabial nonsyllabic vocoid' and as a 'back vocoid' -- presumably it is labial-velar.

Sources: 
Post, U.R. 1966. The phonology of Tiruray. Philippine Journal of Science 95/3: 563-75.

Schlegel, S.A. 1971. Tiruray-English Lexicon. University of California Press, Berkeley, Los Angeles, London.
