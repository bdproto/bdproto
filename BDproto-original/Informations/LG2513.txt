Language name: TIDDIM CHIN

Language number: 2513

Alternate names: Kamhau

Classification: Sino-Tibetan, Kuki-Naga

Comments:
Tiddim Chin is spoken in Chin Hills, Tiddim area in Burma.

Sources: 
Henderson, E.J. 1965. Tiddim Chin: A Descriptive Analysis of Two Texts. Oxford University Press, London.
