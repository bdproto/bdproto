Language name: BAKAIRI

Language number: 6856

Alternate names: Cura

Classification: South American, Macro-Carib

Comments:
Bakairi is spoken in the Mato Grosso, Brazil. Wheatley, (1969, 1973) provides only sketchy information on the phonology. In particular it is not clear which vowels occur nasalized, but examples are cited with the nasalized vowels shown.

Sources: 
Wheatley, J. 1973. Pronouns and nominal elements in Bacairi discourse. Linguistics 104: 105-115.

Wheatley, J. 1969. Bakairi verb structure. Linguistics 47: 80-100.
