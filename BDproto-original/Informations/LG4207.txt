Language name: NYANGI

Language number: 4207

Alternate names:

Classification: Nilo-Saharan, E. Sudanic

Comments:
Nyangi has two basic level tones; rising and falling patterns apparently surface in final syllables only.  There is no vowel harmony.

Sources: 
Heine, B. 1975a. Tepes und Nyangi: zwei ostafrikanische Restsprachen. Afrika und Ubersee 58: 263-300.
