Language name: JAPRERIA

Language number: 6848

Alternate names: 

Classification: S. American, Macro-Carib

Comments:
/b/ includes bilabial and labial-velar approximant allophones. The central high vowel only occurs in penultimate syllables under weak stress -- it is not clear if it could be regarded as a variant of one of the other vowels.

Sources: 
Durbin, M. and Seijas, H. 1972. The phonological structure of the Western Carib languages of the Sierra de Perija, Venezuela. Atti del XL Congresso Internalzionale degli Americanisti. vol. III: 69-77.
