Language name: KONYAGI

Language number: 4145

Alternate names: Basari

Classification: Niger-Kordofanian, West Atlantic

Comments:
Konyagi has two level tones and contours derived from their combination. 'Schwa' and 'ash' are weak vowels after which consonants are lengthened; the remaining vowels may appear long under predictable conditions.

Sources: 
Santos, R. 1977. Phonologie et Morphotonologie de la Langue W~ey~ (Konyagi). Les Langues Africaines au Senegal no. 69. Centre de Linguistique Appliquee de Dakar.
