Language name: Iai

Language number: 2422

Alternate names: Iaai

Classification: Austro-Tai, E. Malayo-Polynesian

Comments:
Iai is spoken in Ouvea Island, Loyality Islands in New Caledonia.

Sources: 
Haudricourt, A.G. 1971. New Caledonia and the Loyalty Islands. Current Trends in Linguistics (T. Sebeok ed.) 8: 359-396.

Ozanne-Rivierre, F. 1976. Le Iaai. SELAF, Paris.

Tryon, D.T. 1968. Iai Grammar (Pacific Linguistics, Series B, 8). Australian National University, Canberra.
