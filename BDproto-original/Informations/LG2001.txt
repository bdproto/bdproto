Language name: IRISH

Language number: 2001

Alternate names: 

Classification: Indo-European, Celtic, Goidelic

Comments:
Irish  is spoken in the Western Isles northwest and southwestcoasts, Galway, part of Mayo, Kerry, Donegal, Ireland. The voiceless nasals and liquids can be interpreted as clusters with /h/.
