Language name: KOHUMONO

Language number: 4136

Alternate names: 

Classification: Niger-Kordofanian, Cross River

Comments:
Kohumono is tentatively analyzed as having three level tones.  /i,e,@,o,u/ form one vowel harmony set in opposition to the other vowels.  Vowel harmony appears to be based on tongue root position.

Sources: 
Cook, T.L. 1969. Some tentative notes on the KoHumono language.  Rsearch Notes (Dept. of Linguistics and Nigerian Languages, University of Ibadan) 2.3:1-49.
