Language name: WIK-MUNKAN

Language number: 8358

Alternate names: 

Classification: Australian, Pama-Nyungan

Comments:
Non-glottal stops have voiced allophones following nasals and (usually) intervocalically. All five vowels occur long and short.

Sources: 
McConnel, U.H. 1945. Wikmunkan phonetics. Oceania 15/4: 353-375.

Sayers, B. and Godfrey, M. 1964. Outline description of the alphabet and grammar of a dialect of Wik-Munkan, spoken at Coen, North Queensland. Occasional Papers in Aboriginal Studies, Canberra. no. 2: 49-65.
