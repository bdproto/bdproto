Language name: MURSI

Language number: 4214

Alternate names: Murle

Classification: Nilo-Saharan, E. Sudanic

Comments:
The language is tonal but the system is not well understood.

Sources: 
Arensen,  J. 1982. Murle Grammar. Occasional Papers in the Study of Sudanese Languages, no. 2.

Turton, D. and Bender, M.L. 1976. Mursi. In M.L. Bender (ed.), The Non-Semitic Languages of Ethiopia. African Studies Center, Michigan State University, East Lansing: 533-62.
